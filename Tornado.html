What

A Tornado is a rapidly roatating column of air that is in contact with both the surface of the Earth and a funnel cloud or, in rare cases, thr base of a funnel cloud. Tornadoes could cause death, injury, and damage to homes, the environment and humans depending on the severity of the tornado
