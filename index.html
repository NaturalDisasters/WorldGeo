All About Natural Disasters!

Natural Diasasters are major storms or events that are produced through the natural proccesses happening on earth.
From atmopsheric conditions forming deadly storms to geological conditions forming deadly volcanos or earthquakes and even further, These catastrophic events can occur in many ways.
These dangerous disasters occur all over the world and are classified through different scales and characteristics.
