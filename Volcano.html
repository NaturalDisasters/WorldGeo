Volcanos

A volcano is a rupture in the crust of a planetary-mass object, such as Earth, that allows hot lava, volcanic ash, and gases to escape from a magma chamber below the surface. A volcano is a mountain that opens downward to a reservoir of molten rock below the surface of the earth.
